Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

Data Collection

WallFlare does not collect any personal data. All preferences (favorites, history, settings) are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

Local Storage

We use localStorage to save your favorites, download history, and theme preferences. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Wallpapers are fetched from Blogger API. No user data is shared with any third party.
